Narative Visualisation     

Examining the trend towards a more urbanised society

Since detailed records have been taken, there has been a general trend towards a more urbanised way of living. The data presented within this visualisation (from the World Bank dataset) details the percentage growth rate in urban population versus the urban population percentage for the years 2005, 2010 and 2015 for all the available countries of the world. Some minor data cleaning has been completed.


This Interactive Slideshow visualisation allows the user to step through the three available years while providing the ability to explore the data at each time point. The visualisation also provides some annotations detailing some interesting findings within the data.
